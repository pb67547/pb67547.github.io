Personal Life

I was born in Atlanta, Georgia on July 26, 1999. My family includes me, my mom, my dad, and my two older sisters. My dad is from Colombia and my mom is from South Georgia so I experienced different types of cultures growing up. When I was five, my family moved from Suwanee to Peachtree City. I stayed there until I graduated from high school and went off to college.


Academics

I attended Starr's Mill High School in Fayetteville, Georgia. During my time at Starr's Mill, I was involved in several extra-curricular activites. I was the president of FBLA and captain of the varsity soccer team. I was also a member of Beta Club, National Honor Society, and Spanish Club. Upon graduating from Starr's Mill, I went on to attend the University of Georgia which is where I currently live and study. I am majoring in Finance with hopes of being a financial planner one day!
